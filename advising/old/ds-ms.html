DATA SCIENCE 3+1

BACHELORS AND MASTERS OF SCIENCE DUAL DEGREE

Three (3) undergraduate courses in Data Science, one (1) undergraduate course in Computer Science, two (2) electives, and eight (8) required graduate courses in Data Science. Electives may be courses with either a DATA or CS prefix, pre-approved courses from other departments, or courses approved in consultation with your major advisor.

Students must take 

  1. CS   151 : Intro to Programming in Python
  
  2. DATA 151 : Intro to Data Science in R
  
  3.  Statistics Requirement, choose one of  
      
	
    • DATA 152 : Inferential Statistics
	
    • MATH 138 : Introduction to Applied
 Statistics

    
  
  4. MATH 280 : Math for Data Science*


*Requires Calculus credit.

There are a few courses that Dual Degree students should not take because they are redundant with graduate level coursework.

Students should not take 
  • DATA 252 : Models and Machine Learning
  
  • DATA 351 : Data Management with SQL 
  
  • DATA 352W: Ethics, Teamwork, Communications 


Of note, students must then ensure they are meeting the undergraduate writing requirement through other means (than DATA 352W).
