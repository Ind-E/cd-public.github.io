College Requirements

Graduation requirements include:

  • 5x General Education
    • 2x AH (Arts & Humanities)
    • 3x Sciences
      • 1x MS (Mathematical)
      • 1x NS (Natural)
      • 1x SS (Social)
  • 4x WE
    • 2x NEL (Non-English Language)
    • 2x Any WE, from any of
      • NEL
      • CEL (Community Engaged Learning)
      • CV (Cultural Values)
  • 2x W
    • 1x 300+ W
    • 1x ANY W
  • 1x PDE

As CS/DS major, you will complete 1x MS, 1x 300+ W, and CS majors complete both W requirements.

Courses within AH, SS, WE, and PDE can be counted toward at most two of these requirements. 4 courses without prerequisites are offered next term which count toward all of AH, SS, and PDE, and one of WE or W. 2 are open to 1st & 2nd years only, and two are open to all students.
