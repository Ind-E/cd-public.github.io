40 minutes latter

Find a partner or small group to rotate within.

  • Look for theses:
    • Is there a clear overall thesis, and a thesis for each paragraph?
    • Does the grammatical construction of each sentence support the author's claims?
    • Does each sentence support the thesis in some way?
  • Look for citations:
    • Are the citations convincing?
    • Are the citations inclusive?
    • Are there lines of argument that should be supported by citation?
    • Do citations support the theses?
  • Remember this was written quickly!
