In-Class Writing

We will spend today working on writing.

  • In 40 minutes, write ~750 words on how students at Willamette University Computing & Data Programs can help advance the political program set forward by Blessing Adeoye Jr. for Black inclusion in game development.
  • Include no fewer than three citations.

Then we will peer review in-class.

Some practice or writing is better than no practice or no writing: just do your best!

You may write in any format you like, but you may wish to write in HTML to use elements we have shown in class. Code snippets may count toward your word count if they support you line of argument.

40 minutes latter

Find a partner or small group to rotate within.

  • Look for theses:
    • Is there a clear overall thesis, and a thesis for each paragraph?
    • Does the the writing support the author's claims?
      • Does the grammatical construction of each sentence?
      • Do coding elements?
    • Does each sentence support the thesis in some way?
  • Look for citations:
    • Are the citations convincing?
    • Are the citations inclusive?
    • Are there lines of argument that should be supported by citation?
    • Do citations support the theses?
  • Remember this was written quickly!
