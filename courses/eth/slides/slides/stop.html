Review Question 2

Decision to Stop analysis uses a 95% confidence interval over 148 law enforcement agencies. Suppose 70% of the 148 agencies were found to be unbiased at this confidence level. What is the expected number of false positives?

I am not a statistician, but I think one of these is correct.

  1. 148 * 0.7 * (1 - .95) = 5.18 ~= 5
  2. 148 * 0.3 * (1 - .95) = 2.22 ~= 2
  3. 148 * 1.0 * (1 - .95) = 7.40 ~= 7
